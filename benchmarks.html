Benchmarks

Benchmarking was done for our submission to QEST'23, and the results for those benchmarks are available here. More benchmarks will be available with future submissions.

Benchmarking was done on an NZXT computer running Debian 11 (Linux 5.10) with the following properties:
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen ThreadRipper 16-Core 32 Threads
  • RAM: 64 GiB DDR4
  • Graphics Card: SAPPHIRE
